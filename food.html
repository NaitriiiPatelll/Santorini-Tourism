Khaman


Do you think Khaman and Dhokla are the same? Well! You are wrong here. Although these two delicacies share many similarities, they are not the same.A great balance of saltiness and sweetness can be explored in every bite of Khaman.

Fafda


Fafda is another culinary delight from the state of Gujarat. These long rectangular leaves of chickpea dough are seasoned with ajwain or carom seeds and turmeric, giving it a bright yellow color.

The Gujarati Pakoras!

Gota is Gujarat's very own pakoda dish made from gram flour and fenugreek leaves. Originating from the village of Dakor in Gujarat, Gota is a traditional Gujarati dish and a special delicacy during Holi.

Thepla

The perfect balance of fresh ingredients in the dough imparts a vibrant flavor and unique feel to these thin parathas. The distinct taste of Theplas can be intensified with the accompaniment of chunda or curd.
